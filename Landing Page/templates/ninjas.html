Ninjas

Historically, the word ninja was not in common use, and a variety of regional colloquialisms evolved
to describe what would later be dubbed ninja. Along with shinobi, some examples include monomi ("one who sees"),
nokizaru ("macaque on the roof"), rappa ("ruffian"), kusa ("grass") and Iga-mono ("one from Iga").[8] In historical documents, shinobi is almost always used.
